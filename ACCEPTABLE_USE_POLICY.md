# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

This policy sets rules for acceptable use of the Janus_PQC repository and artifacts.

## Allowed
- Internal evaluation and testing by authorized users.
- Security research within legal and ethical boundaries.

## Prohibited
- Any illegal activity or violation of laws and regulations.
- Unauthorized redistribution, sublicensing, publication, or commercial use.
- Reverse engineering, decompiling, or disassembly beyond what is explicitly permitted by law.
- Development of offensive tooling or exploit frameworks using this code.
- Use in environments claiming FIPS compliance without validated modules.

## Compliance
- Users must comply with applicable export controls and government regulations.
- Do not store or transmit secrets or private keys in issue trackers or logs.

## Enforcement
- Violations may result in access revocation and reporting to relevant authorities.
